Movement: Arrow keys on keyboard
Menu keybind: M

Starting a New game:

Press "N" to start a game of snake


Saving High Scores:

Press "H" to open the High score window. 

enter your name and press save, otherwise it wont save your score.

Whenever you save your name to the high score menu, it instead creates a text file that is updated after you save the high score again. This way you can keep track of your high scores over time.

Whenever you are done on the high score window you can press the "Enter" key to exit the high score window.

Quitting the application:

To exit the game you can press Q or just press the exit button in the top right corner of the screen.
